Chicken Tikka Masala

The national dish of the United Kingdom

Country of Origin : India/UK

This Indian dish has evolved into one of the most popular curries in history.

Ingredients List

  • 2 chicken breasts, cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp ginger, minced
  • 1 tsp cayenne
  • 1 clove garlic


  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, chopped fine
  • ½ jalapeno, seeded and chopped fine
  • ½ tsp ginger, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • ½ tsp cardamom, ground
  • 1/8 tsp clove, ground
  • 1/8 tsp cinnamon, ground
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp coriander, ground
  • ½ tsp chili powder
  • ¼ tsp tumeric
  • ¼ tsp black pepper


  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 14 oz crushed tomato
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 6 tbsp of cream
  • 1 bunch of cilantro, for garnish

Directions

  1. Mix the first set of ingredients with the chicken as a marinade. Let sit at least 1 hour. If Marinating over night, omit lemon juice until the final hour.
  2. Cook chicken on a grill pan in the oven or on a bbq until cooked through.
  3. In a large sauce pan on low add 2 tbsp oil, onion, jalapeno, garlic, and ginger. Saute until soft.
  4. Add above spices, sugar, and &frac18 tsp of both cinnamon and cloves. Fold in tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add crushed tomatoes, bring to simmer, then remove and blend it all together.
  6. Again bring to a low simmer, adding 2 tbsp of yogurt.
  7. Last, add chick and 6 tbsp of cream, stir and serve over rice or with naan.
  8. Garnish with cilantro on top.

Notes

To add pep to your rice, throw in some cardamom seeds or saffron to your rice cooker for added effect. Also, a ¼ tsp turmeric will give the rice a nice yellow color and subtle flavor
