Pasta Puttanesca

The Prostitute's Pasta

Country of Origin : Italy

This easy to make dish utilizes only pantry items for a tasty, aromatic meal.

Ingredients List

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3-5 cloves garlic, smashed and peeled
  • 3-5 anchovy fillets
  • 1 28-ounce can whole plum tomatoes
  • ½ cup pitted black olives
  • 2 tbsp capers
  • crushed red pepper flakes, to taste
  • 1 lb linguine or other long pasta
  • chopped basil, parsley, oregano, or majoram as garnish
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Bring pot of water to boil, salt it.
  2. Warm 2 tbsp oil with garlic and anchovies in skillet until garlic is lightly golden.
  3. Drain tomatoes and crush with hands. Add to skillet with salt and pepper. Raise heat and cook 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in olives, capers, and red peper flakes.
  5. Cook pasta al dente, drain, and mix with sauce and remaining oil.
  6. Garnish with herbs and serve.

Notes

This dish, allegedly invented by prostitutes, is said to lure in customers with its aroma. Puttanesca is a quick meal meant for those who wish to eat and move on to other things.
