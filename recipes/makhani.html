Paneer Makhani

Cheese, Butter Masala - Restaurant Style

Country of Origin : India

Paneer Makhani, also referred to as Paneer Butter Masala, is a tomato and butter sauce flavored by herbs and nuts.

Ingredients List

  • 2 medium onions
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 200 grams Paneer
  • 2 tbsp cashews
  • ¾ dry fenugreek leaves (Kasuri Methi)


  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 2 tsp coriander
  • ¾ tsp chili powder
  • ¼ tumeric


  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp canola or other oil
  • whole garam masala (1 inch cinnamon, 2 cloves, 1 cardamom)


  • ¼ fresh cream
  • chopped coriander leaves, to garnish

Paste Preperations

  • Onion Paste : Chop and boil onion 1o minutes, grind to paste.


  • Cashew Nut Paste : soak 2 tbsp in hot water 15 minutes, grind.


  • Ginger Garlic Paste : grind 100 grams ginger, 100 g garlic, and add 1 tbsp oil. Mix and store.


Directions

  1. Heat butter and oil, add whole garam masala.
  2. When it sputters, add onion paste and ginger garlic paste. Cook until golden brown.
  3. Add cashew nut paste and saute for a few minutes until aroma intensifies.
  4. Add tomato puree and all the spice powders. Cook until raw tomato smell lessens, leaving oil.
  5. Meanwhile, fry paneer in 2-3 tsp oil until slightly brown. Add to tomato mixture.
  6. Add ½ cup water,
    7. fenugreek, and coriander leaves. Simmer for a few minutes.
  7. Garnish with cream, serve with rice, roti, or naan.

Notes

The added cream gives a richness to this dish but is optional. For a healthier option, omit the added cream.
