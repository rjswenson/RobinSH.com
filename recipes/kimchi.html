Kimchi Bokum Bap

Kimchi Fried Rice

Country of Origin : Korea

A quick and easy Korean favorite. Great for a quick lunch.

Ingredients List

  • 1 cup shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tbsp scallions, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup kimchi, chopped
  • ½ - 1 cup kimchi juice
  • 3 cups cooked white sticky rice
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • cheese or panfried egg, optional

Directions

  1. Heat oil in large pan over medium-high heat. Add mushrooms and scallions, 3-5 minutes.
  2. Add Kimchi, cook another 3-5 minutes.
  3. Add Kimchi juice and rice, stir.
  4. Add seseme oil and seeds along with a pinch of salt.
  5. Optionally, sprinkle cheese on top and serve with a fried egg.

Notes

This Korean staple goes well with a mild cheese such as Mozzarella. A can of tuna or ham cubes also add well to Kimchi Bocum Bap.
