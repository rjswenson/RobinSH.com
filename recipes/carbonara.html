Pasta Carbonara

Cheese, Egg, and Oil Sauce

Country of Origin : Italy

This rich, tasty dish will fill your belly without any cream.

Ingredients List

  • 50 grams Pecorino-Romano Cheese, grated
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 170 grams Guanciale, Pancetta, or Bacon
  • 170 grams (6 oz) dried pasta
  • 2 slow cooked eggs, to top
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Boil water with salt and cook pasta to al dente.
  2. Combine Pecorino, egg, olive oil, and black pepper in a bowl. Whisk well.
  3. Chop Guanciale into small strips, fry at medium high until cooked.
  4. Drain hot pasta and add to egg mixture. Egg mixture should thicken into a sauce and stick to the pasta.
  5. Toss with Guanciale.
  6. Plate and top with slow cooked egg.

Notes

The key to this dish is hot pasta. The steaming hot pasta will cook the egg quickly, binding the sauce and pasta together. Also, if Pecorino is hard to come by Parmigiano-Reggiano will do in a pinch.
